Why It Matters

1) Solves the dumb data problem

Embedding semantics (type, context, links, confidence, embeddings) turns static data into interpretable knowledge.

2) Unifies fragmented systems

A single layer for APIs, databases, AI models, and people — like an HTTP for intelligent data.

3) Hybrid reasoning

Blends symbolic structure with neural vectors for reliable, context‑aware results.

4) Private/local AI

Make enterprise data AI model‑ready offline and on‑prem without sending it out.

5) Ecosystem potential

A clear spec invites SDKs, adapters, visualization, KG tools, and vector stores.

6) Philosophical elegance

Data that knows what it is, where it came from, and how sure it is.

7) Self‑describing future

Files/APIs that explain themselves to AI enable interoperable intelligence.
