Licensing

Open where possible. Respect for third‑party rights.

Overview

This page summarizes how AionScript approaches licensing and third‑party materials referenced by or used with the project. It is informational only and does not constitute legal advice.

Third‑Party Frameworks and Content

Based on our internal guidance, framework documentation such as Tailwind CSS is licensed under permissive open‑source terms (e.g., MIT for Tailwind CSS). In contrast, commercial offerings like Tailwind UI or Tailwind Plus have separate proprietary licenses. Our policy is to ground examples and answers in public, open‑licensed documentation and to avoid distributing proprietary UI/Plus snippets.

Retrieval, Citations, and Safety

  • When providing answers derived from third‑party docs, include citations back to the canonical source whenever feasible.
  • Prefer official documentation, public guides, and open‑licensed examples over paid or proprietary content.
  • Do not reproduce or mirror commercial UI/asset code from third‑party vendors.

Generated Outputs

Code or text generated by tools integrating AionScript should be treated like any other authored work: you are responsible for ensuring your use complies with applicable licenses and terms of the referenced sources.

Trademarks

“Tailwind CSS”, “Tailwind UI”, and other names are trademarks of their respective owners. AionScript is not affiliated with or endorsed by those projects unless explicitly stated. For AionScript brand usage, see Trademark Policy.
